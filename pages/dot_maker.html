Dot Maker

Instructions

Create dots by clicking anywhere in the box above.
1. Selecting a number from the drop down and pressing "Remove" will remove a dot created in the order it was created.
2. Pressing "Last" will remove the most recent dot created.
3. Pressing "Undo" will undo any dots created or removed.
4. Pressing "Redo" will revert any changes made by undo.
